About Me

GAME DEVELOPER BASED IN EUROPE
Started out as a Level Designer back in Ubisoft Singapore, I was involved in the creation of the recent Assassin's Creed titles, such as Syndicate, Origins, Odyssey and Valhalla. During my time in Ubisoft SG, I also lead and mentor level designers within the SEA studios which includes Singapore, Chengdu and Philippines.
In July 2021, I embarked on a new journey to Stockholm in Sweden where I joined Starbreeze Studios as Lead Level Designer for their most popular franchise, Payday. I am involved in both Payday 2 DLC heists and the upcoming Payday 3. I lead and manage the Level Design department spread across two projects and also driving the level design direction of the games we are developing in the studio.
During my free time (when not playing games), I am also learning front-end development from the SheCodes workshops and Swedish at SFI.
